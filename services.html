The Six Disciplines

Marine Ceramic Coating

Marine PPF

Self-healing polyurethane film. Primary physical defense for hull and high-impact zones. 36–60 months.

Gelcoat Correction & Polishing

Multi-stage machine correction — removes oxidation, swirl marks, and surface defects. Required base for any protection layer.

Yacht Detailing

Three-tier packages mapped to oxidation level — from premium maintenance wash to full 15-stage restoration.
05 →

Marine Tint

UV-rejecting film for glass and acrylic surfaces. Reduces solar heat, glare, and interior UV exposure year-round.
06 →

Teak & Interior Care

Specialist treatment for teak decks, leather, stainless, and interior surfaces. Preservation without aggressive stripping.
